RISE FOR JUSTICE

Donghak Peasant Rebellion • Korea • 1894

END CORRUPTION • PROTECT THE PEOPLE

Message to the People: We are farmers, workers, and families pushed to the edge. The officials take our rice, our wages, and our dignity. We rise not for chaos — we rise for fairness.

Why We Rebel

  • Corrupt local officials abuse power and steal from villages.
  • Unfair taxes keep peasants trapped in poverty.
  • Tonghak believers face persecution and discrimination.
  • A system built for elites ignores ordinary people.

Our Vision (The World We Demand)

  • Equal treatment and dignity for every person.
  • Honest government that serves the people, not itself.
  • Protection of our communities and our beliefs.
  • Justice that reaches the countryside — not only the capital.

How We Fought

  • We organized local militias and rose together village by village.
  • We took key towns to force the government to listen.
  • We fought major clashes like Hwangtojae and held Jeonju.
  • When foreign armies arrived, our struggle was crushed with greater force.

Call to Action

Stand with the people. Spread the truth. Refuse corruption. Support reform, fairness, and the right to live with dignity.

Choose the people — not the thieves in office.

Enemy
